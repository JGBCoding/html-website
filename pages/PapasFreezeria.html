Papa's Freezeria

Developer: Flipline Studios

Release date: August 5, 2011

Genre: Simulation

Game available at: Cool Math Games(flash version) | Android | Steam

About the game:

Papa's Freezeria is a simulation game where players manage a frozen yogurt shop, taking orders, making treats, and satisfying customers.

What I liked about the game:

The game is relaxing and has nice progression as you unlock new recipes and tools to enhance your shop.
