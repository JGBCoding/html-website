Plants vs. Zombies

Developer: PopCap Games

Release date: March 13, 2009

Genre: Tower Defense

Game available at: Steam

About the game:

Plants vs. Zombies is a tower defense game where players plant various types of plants to defend their home from an invasion of zombies.

What I liked about the game:

Simple to understand but slowly increases the number of options for defense while introducing new obstacles as the game progresses.
